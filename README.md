### Engineering Principles

* **Simplicity over abstraction:** Complex systems fail complexly. I heavily favor readable, maintainable architecture over "clever" engineering.
* **Observability is a feature:** You cannot scale what you cannot see. Metrics, tracing, and structured logging must be built-in from day one.
* **Pragmatic scaling:** Design the architecture to be horizontally scalable from the start, but delay heavy algorithmic optimization until proven by production bottlenecks.

### Systems I Build

* **High-Throughput Backends:** Designing event-driven platforms (Go/Python/Kafka) capable of processing millions of async tasks without deadlocking.
* **Data Infrastructure:** Architecting distributed ETL pipelines and analytics platforms using Airflow, ClickHouse, and Postgres.
* **AI Backend Infrastructure:** Exploring and integrating vector databases and scalable backend patterns to support AI-driven features and RAG pipelines.
